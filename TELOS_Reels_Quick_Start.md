### Day 3-5: Filming
Pick your approach:

**Option A: Use Bloom Assets Only**
- Use generated Bloom images as primary visuals
- Faster turnaround (1-2 days to edit)
- Highest production value (AI-generated, professional aesthetic)
- Recommended if timeline is tight

**Option B: Film Real Footage + Bloom Assets**
- Film actual clinic consultations, athlete training
- Use Bloom for additional variety/b-roll
- More authentic representation
- Longer timeline (3-5 days filming, plus editing)

**Option C: Hybrid Approach**
- Use Bloom for hero shots (lab, clinic interior)
- Film close-ups of your real team/patients
- Best of both worlds (authenticity + polish)
- Moderate timeline

**Your Decision:** Which approach fits your timeline/resources?

## COMPLIANCE REMINDER

✅ **These Reels are compliant because they:**
- Focus on clinic services, assessment, personalization
- Emphasize science-based, data-driven approach
- Use patient testimonials (with consent)
- Feature educational content
- Avoid specific product/medication claims
- Use only approved voiceover language

❌ **Never add:**
- Specific peptide names or brands
- Health/performance claims about products
- Therapeutic language
- Implied benefits without evidence
- Unauthorized testimonials
