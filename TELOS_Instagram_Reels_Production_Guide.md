# TELOS WELLNESS & LIFESTYLE
## Instagram Reels Production Guide
**4-Reel Campaign: Athletic Performance & Luxury Wellness**

---

## BRAND GUIDELINES (Quick Reference)

**Brand Name:** TELOS  
**Target Audience:** Adults 21+, athletic/performance-focused, luxury wellness positioning  
**Tone:** Sophisticated, exclusive, science-backed, aspirational  
**Aesthetic:** Premium, minimal, high-contrast, luxury lifestyle  
**Call-to-Action:** None (brand presence focus)  
**Primary Platform:** Instagram Reels (9:16 format)

---

## REEL 1: THE SCREENING
### High-Level Concept
Introduce TELOS's exclusive assessment methodology. Emphasize precision matching and personalized protocols.

### Technical Specifications
- **Duration:** 25 seconds
- **Aspect Ratio:** 9:16 (1080x1920px)
- **Frame Rate:** 30fps
- **Color Grade:** Cool, clinical luxury (blues/grays, high contrast)
- **Music:** Subtle, modern, minimalist (recommendation: lo-fi instrumental, 80-100 BPM)

### Script & Voiceover
```
"Your body is unique. So should your protocol.

We don't prescribe the same solution to everyone. First comes the 
comprehensive assessment—blood work, metabolic analysis, 
performance benchmarks.

Then we match you with what actually works for you.

Precision wellness."
```

### Shot-by-Shot Breakdown

| Time | Duration | Visual | Notes |
|------|----------|--------|-------|
| 0:00-0:03 | 3s | Close-up of premium lab equipment, clean, minimal lighting | Focus on precision, luxury clinical setting |
| 0:03-0:08 | 5s | Patient in consultation room with TELOS practitioner reviewing data on screen | Sophisticated, well-lit, high-end furniture |
| 0:08-0:15 | 7s | Split screen montage: blood draw, metabolic testing, biometric scanning, lab results on tablet | Emphasize comprehensive assessment |
| 0:15-0:22 | 7s | Practitioner presenting personalized protocol to patient, pointing to screen, confident posture | Patient looks satisfied/engaged |
| 0:22-0:25 | 3s | TELOS logo/brand mark with "Precision Wellness" text, fade to black | Clean, luxury reveal |

### Key Visual Elements
- Premium lab setting (clinical but upscale)
- Confident practitioners in professional attire
- High-quality medical/testing equipment
- Patient appears engaged, data-focused
- Cool color palette with chrome/glass accents

### Content Production Notes
- Shoot in TELOS clinic with best lighting (north-facing windows or professional lighting)
- Use real lab equipment, real patients (with consent)
- Multiple angle: wide shots of room, close-ups of tech/screens, practitioner-patient interaction
- Capture genuine consultation moments, not staged
- Color grade for luxury (crush blacks slightly, boost clarity)

## REEL 2: ATHLETE SUCCESS STORY
### High-Level Concept
Real-world transformation narrative. From plateau to breakthrough using data-driven optimization.

### Technical Specifications
- **Duration:** 20 seconds
- **Aspect Ratio:** 9:16 (1080x1920px)
- **Frame Rate:** 30fps
- **Color Grade:** Warm, energetic with brand color accents
- **Music:** Motivational but sophisticated (not hype—premium performance vibe, 110-130 BPM)

### Script & Voiceover
```
"6 months ago: Hitting a wall in performance.

Plateau for 2 years. Training harder wasn't working.

After assessment and protocol optimization: new PRs, better recovery, 
competitive edge restored.

This is what happens when science meets ambition."
```

### Shot-by-Shot Breakdown

| Time | Duration | Visual | Notes |
|------|----------|--------|-------|
| 0:00-0:04 | 4s | Athlete looking frustrated at gym, slow-motion workout footage, struggling movement | Show struggle/plateau clearly |
| 0:04-0:08 | 4s | Transition to TELOS clinic consultation, practitioner and athlete reviewing assessment results on screen | Shift in mood/energy |
| 0:08-0:14 | 6s | Montage of athlete training (faster cuts): lifting heavy, explosive speed work, conditioning, focused intensity | High energy, clear improvement |
| 0:14-0:18 | 4s | Achievement moment: setting personal record, celebration, competitive success, or physical breakthrough | Peak moment—genuine emotion |
| 0:18-0:20 | 2s | Athlete + TELOS practitioner, thumbs up, satisfied nod, confident look; TELOS logo | Closure, partnership visual |

### Key Visual Elements
- Real athlete (same person before/after scenario)
- Actual training environment (gym, track, sport-specific setting)
- Fast-paced cuts during "success" section
- Genuine emotional moments (frustration, then satisfaction)
- Professional achievement visual (PR board, competition, metric validation)

### Content Production Notes
- Partner with 1-2 local athletes willing to participate
- Film at actual training location (their gym/sport)
- Get slow-motion of "struggling" phase, regular speed of success phase
- Capture actual PRs or competition moments if possible
- Warm color grade, slightly increase saturation in success section

## REEL 3: DAY IN THE LIFE
### High-Level Concept
Show the comprehensive, integrated nature of TELOS optimization across the full day.

### Technical Specifications
- **Duration:** 30 seconds
- **Aspect Ratio:** 9:16 (1080x1920px)
- **Frame Rate:** 30fps
- **Color Grade:** High-end, saturated, aspirational (premium lifestyle content)
- **Music:** Modern, upbeat but refined (think luxury brand soundtrack, 100-120 BPM)

### Script & Voiceover
```
"A day optimizing performance.

Morning: Recovery protocol.

Mid-day: Personalized nutrition adjustment.

Evening: Biometric tracking update.

Wellness isn't a destination. It's a system.

This is luxury athletic care."
```

### Shot-by-Shot Breakdown

| Time | Duration | Visual | Notes |
|------|----------|--------|-------|
| 0:00-0:05 | 5s | Morning scene: patient doing recovery work (foam rolling, stretching, mobility work) in clean, modern home gym/space | Peaceful, intentional, upscale environment |
| 0:05-0:10 | 5s | TELOS clinic scene: nutrition consultation, meal plan review, practitioner discussing personalized protocol | Professional, informative, one-on-one care |
| 0:10-0:18 | 8s | Biometric tracking sequence: patient using wearable device, data visualization on phone app, heart rate/sleep/recovery metrics animated on screen | Tech-forward, data-driven |
| 0:18-0:25 | 7s | Transition montage: patient hydrating, checking app, eating prepared meal, professional environment, morning sunlight | Integrated lifestyle moments |
| 0:25-0:30 | 5s | Split-screen finale showing all three elements (recovery, nutrition, tracking) simultaneously, TELOS logo with "Systems-Based Wellness" text | Closure, brand mark |

### Key Visual Elements
- Premium home environment (well-designed, minimal, modern)
- TELOS clinic environment (professional, clean, premium)
- Wearable tech visible (Apple Watch, Oura Ring, etc.)
- App interface visible on phone (data dashboards, metrics)
- High production value throughout (professional lighting, color grading)

### Content Production Notes
- Scout luxury home gym spaces or use TELOS clinic
- Capture real wearable devices and app interfaces
- Get close-ups of mobility work (foam rolling, stretching)
- Film clinic nutrition consultation with real practitioner and patient
- Shoot at different times of day (morning light, midday, evening)
- Saturate colors slightly, boost contrast for aspirational feel

## REEL 4: MYTH VS. REALITY
### High-Level Concept
Educational content that frames TELOS as the premium, scientific alternative to generic fitness advice.

### Technical Specifications
- **Duration:** 22 seconds
- **Aspect Ratio:** 9:16 (1080x1920px)
- **Frame Rate:** 30fps
- **Color Grade:** Myth section desaturated; Reality section bright, clear, high-contrast
- **Music:** Sophisticated, modern, slight educational undertone (120-140 BPM)

### Script & Voiceover
```
"MYTH: Just train harder and eat clean.

REALITY: Performance optimization is hormonal, metabolic, and systemic. 
One-size-fits-all doesn't work.

THE DIFFERENCE: Data-driven, personalized protocols that actually 
move the needle.

This is the level of care elite athletes deserve."
```

### Shot-by-Shot Breakdown

| Time | Duration | Visual | Notes |
|------|----------|--------|-------|
| 0:00-0:05 | 5s | Generic gym training montage (quick cuts, slightly muted/faded look): standard exercises, generic athletes, repetitive movements | Represent "common approach"—well-meaning but generic |
| 0:05-0:10 | 5s | Text overlay animation: "MYTH: Just train hard" with athlete looking frustrated or plateaued | Shift visual mood, introduce problem |
| 0:10-0:15 | 5s | Transition to TELOS clinic: clean lab, professional environment, bright/clear aesthetic, organized space | Dramatic visual shift |
| 0:15-0:20 | 5s | Text overlay animation: "REALITY: Personalized science" with data visualization (blood work results, metabolic panels, personalized protocol on screen, graphs) | Technical sophistication on display |
| 0:20-0:22 | 2s | TELOS logo + "Elite Performance, Optimized" text on clean background | Brand closure |

### Key Visual Elements
- Myth section: generic gym footage, de-saturated, slightly blurred
- Reality section: TELOS clinic, sharp, saturated, professional
- Data visualizations: actual lab results, graphs, metrics on screens
- Text overlays: bold, modern, high-contrast
- Lighting shift: myth = dim/flat; reality = bright/professional

### Content Production Notes
- Use generic gym stock footage for Myth section (or film generic exercises)
- Desaturate Myth footage in post (reduce saturation by 30-40%)
- Film Reality section in TELOS clinic with professional lighting
- Capture actual lab result screens, metabolic analysis dashboards
- Create animated text overlays in post-production
- Use color grading to emphasize shift from generic to premium

## PRODUCTION TIMELINE

**Phase 1: Asset Generation (Days 1-2)**
- Generate Bloom visuals for all 4 Reels
- Collect/organize reference materials

**Phase 2: Filming (Days 3-5)**
- Schedule clinic shoots (consultations, interior, lab)
- Schedule athlete/patient shoots (training, achievement)
- Capture supplemental b-roll (lifestyle, home gym, wearables)

**Phase 3: Post-Production (Days 6-8)**
- Color grade and edit all footage
- Add voiceover/music
- Create text overlays and animations
- Format for 9:16 Instagram Reels

**Phase 4: Review & Publishing (Days 9+)**
- Internal review and refinement
- Publish to Instagram Reels
- Monitor engagement and iterate

---

## DISTRIBUTION STRATEGY

**Publishing Schedule:**
- Publish 1 Reel every 2-3 days (stagger them)
- Optimal posting times: Tues-Thurs, 10am-2pm, 7-9pm

**Cross-Promotion:**
- Repurpose clips to Stories
- Link to longer-form content (blog, podcast)
- Highlight testimonials from featured athletes/patients

**Engagement Tactics:**
- Respond to comments quickly (luxury/premium positioning)
- Use Reels Insights to identify top-performing moments
- A/B test music/pacing variations

## COMPLIANCE NOTES

✅ **What's Included:**
- Focus on clinic services and screening process
- Emphasis on personalized, data-driven approach
- General mention of "protocol optimization"
- Patient testimonials (with proper consent/releases)
- Educational content about optimization

❌ **What's NOT Included:**
- Specific product/peptide names or features
- Performance claims about specific medications
- Therapeutic language or health claims
- Implied benefits without scientific basis
