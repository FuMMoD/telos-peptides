## POST-PRODUCTION NOTES

### Required Elements Per Reel
- ✅ Voiceover (professional, clear, matching tone)
- ✅ Background Music (see Production Guide for BPM/vibe)
- ✅ Text Overlays (titles, key phrases, brand mark)
- ✅ Transitions (smooth cuts, fades, pattern shifts)
- ✅ Color Grade (per Production Guide specifications)
- ✅ Sound Design (optional: ambient sounds, footsteps, etc.)

### Tools Recommended
- **Video Editing:** Adobe Premiere Pro, CapCut, Final Cut Pro
- **Motion Graphics:** Adobe After Effects, Motion, DaVinci Resolve
- **Color Grading:** DaVinci Resolve, Adobe Premiere Pro
- **Voiceover Recording:** Audition, GarageBand, or professional studio

### Audio Guidelines
- **Voiceover:** Professional, calm, authoritative tone
- **Music:** Royalty-free (Epidemic Sound, Artlist, AudioJungle)
- **Audio Levels:** -6dB to -3dB peak to avoid clipping
- **Mix:** Voiceover should be dominant, music supportive
